Silk Fields

A living textile of vectors and light. Comb the weave, bend its flow, and let color breathe.

Drag to comb the fibers. Click to shuffle the warp. Double‑click toggles pause.

Weave

Threads are tiny travelers in a vector field; the fabric emerges from their memory.

Comb

Your pointer bends local flow—close passes carve swales; gentle arcs leave ripples.

Bloom

Light accumulates additively. Lower fade for long velvet trails; raise it for crisp silk.

Breath

Subtle oscillations keep the textile alive even at rest—quiet, never still.
